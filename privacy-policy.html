MeloTab Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

MeloTab is a Chrome extension that customizes the browser new tab page.

Data we process

1) Local settings data. Theme, weather preferences, shortcuts, todo items, and UI options are stored in Chrome extension storage.

2) Optional geolocation. If enabled by you, location is used for local weather. If disabled, fallback city can be used.

3) Network requests. Weather and reverse geocoding requests are sent to https://api.open-meteo.com and https://geocoding-api.open-meteo.com.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not use third-party analytics trackers.
  • We do not require account registration.

Data retention

Settings are stored in your browser extension storage until reset/removal or extension uninstall.

Your controls

  • You can disable geolocation in settings.
  • You can reset settings at any time.
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove local extension data.
